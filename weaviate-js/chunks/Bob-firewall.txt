*** START OF CHUNK 2
Base Service: The STACK Firewall Service is comprised of only blue firewalls and the supporting firewall management infrastructure, providing additional security controls to government applications and data. The Firewall Service can filter and block based on IP addresses, ports and applications. The Service maintains “state” for all connections and logs connections to a secure database.
